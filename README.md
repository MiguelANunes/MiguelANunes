## Hi, I'm migs

I'm a logician and computer scientist who dabbles in philosophy, mainly philosophy of logic and philosophy of (computer) science.

Currently I'm pursuing a PhD in Philosophy, with a focus on formal logic, at the Institute of Philosophy and Human Sciences of Unicamp, though I spend most of my time at the Center of Logic, Epistemology and History of Science (CLE).

I'm a member of the Research Group on Foundations of Computation (Grupo de Pesquisa em Fundamentos da Computação - Função) from the State University of Santa Catarina (Udesc), we are [here in GitHub](https://github.com/funcao).

I do a lot of stuff with LaTeX, mostly bodges, some stuff with proof assistants, mostly Rocq, and little stuff with code, mostly Python and Shell Scripts.

### My Publications

Ordered from newest to oldest.

- [A Sound Deep Embedding of Arbitrary Normal Modal Logics in Coq](https://doi.org/10.1145/3561320.3561329), published in 2022 and presented @ XXVI Brasilian Symposium on Programming Languages (SBLP22)
